                          MEASURES DISCHARGED

  The following joint resolution was discharged from the Committee on 
Environment and Public Works, by petition, pursuant to 5 U.S.C. 802(c), 
and placed on the calendar:

       S.J. Res. 31. Joint resolution providing for congressional 
     disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, 
     of the rule submitted by the Environmental Protection Agency 
     relating to ``Review of Final Rule Reclassification of Major 
     Sources as Area Sources Under Section 112 of the Clean Air 
     Act''.
