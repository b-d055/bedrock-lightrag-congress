                        TRIBUTE TO ADRIK FISHER

<bullet> Mr. ROUNDS. Mr. President, today I recognize Adrik Fisher, an 
intern in my Washington, DC, office, for all the hard work he has done 
on behalf of my office and the State of South Dakota. Mr. Fisher is 
originally from New Underwood, SD. He is currently attending Patrick 
Henry College, where he studies American politics and policy. Mr. 
Fisher is a dedicated and diligent individual who has been devoted to 
getting the most out of his internship experience. He has been a true 
asset to my office. I extend my sincere thanks and appreciation to Mr. 
Fisher for all of the work he has done and wish him continued success 
in the years to come.<bullet>
