                         MESSAGE FROM THE HOUSE


                          ENROLLED BILL SIGNED

  At 6:31 p.m., a message from the House of Representatives, delivered 
by Mrs. Cole, one of its reading clerks, announced that the Speaker has 
signed the following enrolled bill:

       H.R. 1968. An act making further continuing appropriations 
     and other extensions for the fiscal year ending September 30, 
     2025, and for other purposes.

  The enrolled bill was subsequently signed by the President pro 
tempore (Mr. Grassley).
