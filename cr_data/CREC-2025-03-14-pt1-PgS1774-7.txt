                             Cloture Motion

  Mr. BARRASSO. Mr. President, I send a cloture motion to the desk.
  The PRESIDING OFFICER. The cloture motion having been presented under 
rule XXII, the Chair directs the clerk to read the motion.
  The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:

                             Cloture Motion

       We, the undersigned Senators, in accordance with the 
     provisions of rule XXII of the Standing Rules of the Senate, 
     do hereby move to bring to a close debate on the nomination 
     of Executive Calendar No. 45, Martin Makary, of Virginia, to 
     be Commissioner of Food and Drugs, Department of Health and 
     Human Services.
         John Thune, Tim Sheehy, Mike Crapo, Markwayne Mullin, 
           Joni Ernst, David McCormick, Rick Scott of Florida, 
           Bernie Moreno, Mike Rounds, Tommy Tuberville, Katie 
           Britt, Shelley Moore Capito, Jim Justice, John 
           Barrasso, Steve Daines, Jon Husted.
